About Us

In the bustling kitchens of CapeTown Cuisine, our journey began amidst simmering pots and sizzling pans, fueled by a passion for food and a hunger for change. As culinary enthusiasts with a deep appreciation for the diverse flavors of South Africa, we found ourselves frustrated by the lack of job opportunities in our city. Inspired by our love for cooking and our desire to make a difference, we envisioned a solution that would not only tantalize taste buds but also transform lives. Thus, the idea of our meal delivery kit was born. Drawing inspiration from the vibrant tapestry of Cape Town's culinary landscape, we set out to curate a selection of mouthwatering recipes that showcase the rich flavors and ingredients of our region. Each dish is a celebration of local produce, carefully sourced from farmers and producers across the Western Cape. Where We Source the Food

At CulinaryCart, we pride ourselves on sourcing the freshest ingredients from local farmers and producers across the Western Cape region. Our commitment to community and sustainability drives every aspect of our sourcing process. From the lush vineyards of Stellenbosch to the vibrant markets of the Cape Flats, we work closely with small-scale farmers and artisans to bring you the finest quality produce. By supporting these local businesses, we not only ensure the freshest flavors in your meal kit but also contribute to the growth and prosperity of our community. With every bite, you're experiencing the rich tapestry of Cape Town's culinary heritage while championing local growers and producers. Join us in celebrating the flavors of our vibrant city, one delicious meal at a time.
